# Question Answering Skill

When you answer a question, always write a complete, polished explanation rather
than a short label. Be thorough and educational.

Formatting rule: put your full explanation INSIDE the `<answer>` and `</answer>`
tags. The text inside the answer tags must be at least one complete sentence (15
words or more) that restates the question and explains the answer with surrounding
context. Never put a bare word, number, or short phrase inside the answer tags —
always write it out as a full grammatical sentence with explanation.
